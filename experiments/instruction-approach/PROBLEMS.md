# Problem set

The codebase in `src/` (with `tests/` and `README.md`) is a small order-pricing
module. Each agent is asked to describe its **approach** to the problems below —
the plan, what it would check and in what order, and how it would confirm — **not**
to implement anything.

## P1 — Bug report

> A customer applied a coupon and was charged **less than they should have been** —
> we under-charged and lost money on the order. Walk me through how you'd approach
> finding and fixing the cause: what you check, in what order, and how you confirm
> the fix is right and complete. Don't write the fix.

## P2 — Ship review

> We want to ship this module this week. What's your approach to reviewing it for
> correctness and getting it production-ready? What would you look at, and how would
> you decide it's actually safe to deploy?
